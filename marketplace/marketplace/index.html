Pentaho Marketplace

Pentaho Marketplace allows you to get in contact with plugins and language packs developed by the Pentaho Community.

You can contribute by submitting your plugins and language packs, letting everyone benefit from your work. This is a good way to receive feedback and have your work tested by other community members.

All the work that you can find in the Pentaho Marketplace has a Project Maturity Classification that clarifies what is supported and what is the level of support attached to the project.

Marketplace in Pentaho 6.0+

If you're using the Community Edition of Pentaho 6.0+ you don't have to worry about this - Marketplace comes with it and it's ready to be used.
If you have Pentaho 6.0+ Enterprise Edition and go to Marketplace Public Site to try to download the Marketplace plugin, you'll see it's not there.
The reason for that? It's an OSGi plugin and you already have it, but it comes disabled by default. Here are the instructions on how to enable it:

  1. Edit the file 
    pentaho-solutions/system/karaf/etc/org.apache.karaf.features.cfg
  2. Find the key "featuresBoot" and add the feature "pentaho-marketplace". That line will then be something like:
    featuresBoot=config,management,kar,cxf,camel,camel-blueprint,camel-stream,pentaho-server,mongolap,pentaho-metaverse,pdi-dataservice-client,pentaho-yarn-obf,pentaho-marketplace

Restart your server, and you're done!

Marketplace in Pentaho 5.4

Just unzip the marketplace folder in your /system/ folder, restart your server and you're done.
