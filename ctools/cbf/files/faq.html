Frequently Asked Questions

Build complains about missing xmltask?

You need to download xmltask.jar and put it in your ant lib directory (e.g.: /usr/share/ant/).

The build fails with a lot of errors on junit. How do I fix it?

Same as with xmltask: you need to put junit.jar in your ant lib dir. This is a requirement since Pentaho 3.8.
