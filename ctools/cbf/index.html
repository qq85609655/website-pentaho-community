Focused on a multi-project / multi-environment scenario, CBF is the way to setup and deploy Pentaho-based applications.
CBF is extremely useful when working in several projects at the same time, allowing you to switch among several environments (production, development, etc.), even when customizing the Pentaho server (using different databases, changing the look and feel or applying different security types).
